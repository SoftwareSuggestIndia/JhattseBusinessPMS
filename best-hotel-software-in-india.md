## Functional Highlights

### PMS & Front Desk

* Guest profiles and stay records
* Room availability and occupancy tracking
* Check-in / check-out workflows
* Multiple billing folios per stay

### Distribution & Channels

* Central inventory update workflow
* Rate publishing across connected OTAs
* Manual override support for rate changes

### POS & Food Service

* Order capture and billing
* Kitchen / service order printing or display
* Optional consolidation with room folio billing

### Guest Communication

* Automated booking confirmations
* Post-booking message templates
* Optional broadcast messaging

### Operations & Housekeeping

* Room task assignment list
* Clean / dirty / inspection states
* Status update timestamps
